What is Sustainable Fashion?

Sustainable fashion means choosing clothing that’s kind to people and the planet. Hover over the cards to learn more!

Eco-Friendly Materials

Uses organic, natural, and recycled fabrics that reduce water, energy, and chemical use.

Ethical Production

Promotes fair wages, safe working conditions, and labor transparency throughout the supply chain.

Slow Fashion

Focuses on durability and timeless styles instead of fast-changing fashion trends.

Circular Design

Encourages reusing, repairing, and recycling garments to avoid unnecessary waste.

Minimal Waste

Reduces textile pollution through better design, low-impact dyes, and mindful consumption.

The journey to sustainable fashion doesn’t require a complete wardrobe overhaul. You can start small—buy less, choose well, and make it last.
